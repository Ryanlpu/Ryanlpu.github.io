"Magic card girl Sakura" is a TV animation adapted from the cartoon of the same name. The first season premiered on April 7, 1998 with 35 episodes; the second season premiered on April 13, 1999 with a total of 11 episodes; the third season premiered on September 7, 1999 with a total of 24 episodes. A total of 70 episodes. The animation was produced by madhouse. The animation was first broadcast by NHK satellite channel 2 (now NHK BS premium) in Japan and in Shanghai TV station in October 2002 in mainland China.
